N-gram character models


A sequence of written symbols of length n is called an n-gram with special case “unigram” for 1-gram, “bigram” for 2-gram, and “trigram” for 3-gram. A model of the probability distribution of n-letter sequences is thus called an n-gram model.

IR scoring functions


A scoring function takes a document and a query and returns a numeric score; the most relevant documents have the highest scores. In the BM25 function, the score is a linear weighted combination of scores for each of the words that make up the query. Three factors that affect the weight of a query term are: term frequency, inverse document frequency and length of the document.
